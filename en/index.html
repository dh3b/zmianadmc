GVW change to 2 500 kg

Ideal for urgent and sensitive shipments

We reduce the gross vehicle weight to 2500 kg — the procedure is carried out by an authorised technical institute in Germany, before the vehicle enters your home country. The process concludes with vehicle registration in Germany.

What you receive

  • A complete set of new registration documents (Brief Teil I and II) with GVW = 2500 kg
  • A new rating plate with updated weight values
  • Technical documentation recognised across the EU — ready for re‑registration in any country

Who is this for

  • Vehicles that do not need a 2.5–3.5 t payload capacity
  • Light, urgent and sensitive loads requiring fast transport
  • Companies that value flexibility, speed and zero downtime

Category N1 · category B driving licence

We reduce the gross vehicle weight — the entire process is carried out in Germany, before the vehicle reaches your home country. This service applies to vehicles purchased anywhere in the European Union.

What you receive

  • New German registration documents (Brief Teil I and II) with the updated GVW

Benefits for you

  • Drive the vehicle on a category B driving licence
